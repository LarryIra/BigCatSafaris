Discover the Natural Splendor of Nyungwe Forest with Our 3-Day Exploration Tour

Experience the untouched beauty and diverse wildlife of Nyungwe National Park with our comprehensive 3-day exploration tour. This adventure offers a blend of exhilarating canopy walks, engaging chimpanzee tracking, and immersive forest hikes, providing a thorough exploration of Rwanda’s most pristine rainforest.

Nyungwe Forest National Park

Three-Day Nyungwe Forest Adventure

Explore the biodiversity and beauty of Nyungwe Forest with canopy walks, chimpanzee tracking, and forest hikes over three immersive days.

Arrive at Nyungwe National Park from Kigali and check in at your comfortable lodge or hotel near the park. After settling in, embark on the exciting canopy walkway experience. The walkway, suspended high above the forest floor, offers breathtaking panoramic views of the rainforest and a unique vantage point for observing wildlife. As you traverse the walkway, look out for bird species and monkeys that inhabit the upper reaches of the forest.

In the afternoon, take a leisurely nature walk on one of Nyungwe’s scenic trails. The Igishigishigi Trail or the Waterfall Trail provides additional opportunities to appreciate the park’s diverse flora and fauna. Return to your lodge in the evening for a relaxing dinner and overnight stay.

After breakfast, prepare for an exhilarating chimpanzee tracking adventure. Depart for the park’s headquarters for a briefing on the tracking procedures and safety measures. Set out with your guide on a trek through the lush rainforest to locate the habituated chimpanzee groups. Spend up to one hour observing these fascinating primates in their natural habitat, witnessing their social interactions and behaviors.

Following the chimpanzee tracking, return to your lodge for a well-deserved lunch. In the afternoon, explore other sections of Nyungwe National Park on a guided hike. Consider the Colobus Monkey Trail or the Kamiranzovu Swamp Trail for a chance to see additional wildlife and experience the park’s diverse ecosystems.

Return to your lodge in the evening for dinner and relaxation, reflecting on the day’s discoveries.

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast and embark on a morning forest walk. Choose a trail that interests you, such as the Rukuzi Trail or the Uwinka Viewpoint Trail, for more opportunities to explore Nyungwe’s stunning landscapes and rich biodiversity.

After your morning hike, return to the lodge for lunch. Check out and transfer back to Kigali in the afternoon. Depending on your schedule, you may have time for some last-minute exploration or shopping in Kigali before your departure.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Nyungwe National Park.
  • Guided Tours: Expert guide services for canopy walkway, chimpanzee tracking, and forest hikes.
  • Accommodation: Two nights’ stay in a comfortable lodge or hotel near Nyungwe National Park.
  • Meals: All meals, including lunch and refreshments during your exploration.
  • Park Fees: Entrance fees for Nyungwe National Park, canopy walkway charges, and chimpanzee tracking permits.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Lightweight, breathable clothing suitable for hiking and forest exploration.
  • Hiking Boots: Sturdy footwear for trekking through forest trails.
  • Rain Gear: Waterproof jacket and pants in case of rain.
  • Sun Protection: Hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen for sun protection.
  • Camera & Binoculars: Capture wildlife and stunning views during your adventure.
  • Fitness Level: Moderate fitness is required for hiking and chimpanzee tracking.
  • Permits: Advance booking of chimpanzee tracking permits is required. Ensure reservations are confirmed before your visit.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow health and vaccination guidelines for a safe and enjoyable experience.
