Experience the Best of Rwanda with Our 5-Day Nyungwe National Park and Kigali City Tour

Discover the vibrant culture of Kigali and the lush beauty of Nyungwe National Park with our comprehensive 5-day tour. This package combines an engaging city tour with an in-depth exploration of Nyungwe Forest, including canopy walks, chimpanzee tracking, and a visit to the region’s renowned tea plantations.

Rwanda Discovery Tour

Five-Day Rwanda Cultural and Nature Adventure

Experience Rwanda’s cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty with a comprehensive 5-day tour, including Kigali city exploration, Nyungwe Forest adventures, and more.

Arrive in Kigali, where you will be warmly welcomed by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Begin your exploration with a guided city tour, starting at the Kigali Genocide Memorial to gain insights into Rwanda’s history and resilience. Explore local markets and craft villages to experience Rwandan culture firsthand, and enjoy a delicious lunch featuring traditional cuisine.

In the afternoon, continue your tour by visiting art centers or cultural villages to further immerse yourself in Kigali’s vibrant heritage. Return to your hotel in the evening for dinner and an overnight stay, preparing for the adventure that awaits in Nyungwe Forest.

Depart Kigali for Nyungwe National Park, known for its ancient rainforest and rich biodiversity. Upon arrival, check in at your lodge or hotel near the park. After settling in, embark on the thrilling canopy walkway. Suspended high above the forest floor, this walkway offers panoramic views of the lush canopy and a unique perspective on the park’s flora and fauna.

In the afternoon, take a leisurely nature walk on one of Nyungwe’s scenic trails, such as the Igishigishigi Trail, to further explore the park’s diverse ecosystems. Return to your lodge for dinner and relax in the serene surroundings of Nyungwe Forest.

Start your day with an early breakfast before heading out for an exciting chimpanzee tracking adventure. At the park’s headquarters, receive a briefing on tracking procedures and safety. Trek through the rainforest with your guide to find the habituated chimpanzee groups. Spend up to one hour observing these fascinating primates in their natural habitat.

After your chimpanzee tracking experience, return to the lodge for lunch. In the afternoon, visit the nearby tea plantations in Nyungwe. Learn about the tea production process, from cultivation to processing, and enjoy a tour of the tea fields. This visit provides a unique insight into the local agricultural practices and offers stunning views of the rolling tea plantations.

Return to your lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.

Enjoy a hearty breakfast before embarking on a full day of forest exploration. Choose from various trails, such as the Colobus Monkey Trail or the Kamiranzovu Swamp Trail, to experience different aspects of Nyungwe’s ecosystem. These trails offer opportunities to see additional wildlife and appreciate the park’s breathtaking landscapes.

After a day of hiking and exploration, return to your lodge for a relaxing dinner and evening at leisure.

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast at the lodge before checking out. Transfer back to Kigali, where you may have time for additional exploration or shopping in the city, depending on your flight schedule.

Enjoy a final lunch in Kigali before heading to Kigali International Airport for your departure, concluding your memorable journey through Rwanda’s natural and cultural treasures.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Nyungwe National Park.
  • Guided Tours: Expert guide services for city tour, canopy walkway, chimpanzee tracking, and tea plantation visit.
  • Accommodation: Two nights in a comfortable hotel in Kigali and two nights in a lodge or hotel near Nyungwe National Park.
  • Meals: All meals, including lunch and refreshments during your tour.
  • Park Fees: Entrance fees for Nyungwe National Park, canopy walkway charges, and chimpanzee tracking permits.
  • Tea Plantation Visit: Guided tour of the tea plantations.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Lightweight, breathable clothing suitable for city exploration and forest activities.
  • Hiking Boots: Sturdy footwear for trekking through forest trails.
  • Rain Gear: Waterproof jacket and pants for unpredictable weather.
  • Sun Protection: Hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen.
  • Camera and Binoculars: For capturing wildlife, scenic views, and cultural experiences.
  • Fitness Level: Moderate fitness is required for hiking and chimpanzee tracking.
  • Permits: Advance booking of chimpanzee tracking permits is required. Ensure all reservations are confirmed before your visit.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow recommended health and vaccination guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
