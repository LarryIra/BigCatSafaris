3 Easy Steps

Choose A Destination

Choose the perfect safari destination with our expert recommendations. Based on your interests, budget and travel schedule, we offer personalized suggestions for the best destination to suit your needs. With a variety of destinations ranging from the savannahs of Akagera to the rainforests of Nyungwe, and the mountainous landscapes of Musanze, Rwanda has something for every type of traveler.

Pay Online

Easily complete your safari tour booking by making a secure online payment. Our payment gateway ensures a hassle-free transaction that is both safe and reliable. With instant confirmation and the ability to manage your booking from anywhere in the world. Say goodbye to the hassle of carrying cash or exchanging currency and enjoy a seamless safari adventure experience with us.

Start Your Adventure

Embark on your safari adventure today with our convenient travel options. Our expert team can arrange for seamless travel to your desired destination, so you can start your adventure right away. With a variety of transportation options available, we can help you find the most convenient and cost-effective option to suit your needs. Let us help you make your dream safari a reality today.

Booking

Online Booking

Our easy-to-use booking platform simplifies the reservation process, allowing you to customize your dream safari adventure in Rwanda with just a few clicks. Begin by entering your name, email, preferred date and time, budget, and desired destination.

Once you've provided these details, use the message box to share any special requests or additional information to help us personalize your experience. Our dedicated customer support team is available to address any questions or concerns, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free booking experience. With a seamless combination of convenience and customization, take the first step towards an unforgettable wildlife encounter in the heart of Africa.
