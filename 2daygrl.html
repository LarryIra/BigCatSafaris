Discover the Best of Rwanda with Our 2-Day City Tour and Gorilla Trekking Safari

Experience the vibrant culture of Kigali and the awe-inspiring beauty of Rwanda’s mountain gorillas with our exclusive 2-day safari package. This immersive journey combines a captivating city tour of Kigali with an exhilarating gorilla trekking adventure in Volcanoes National Park, offering you a comprehensive exploration of Rwanda’s natural and cultural treasures.

Safari Experience

2-Day Kigali City Tour & Gorilla Trekking Safari

This short yet powerful 2-day journey blends the cultural vibrance of Kigali with the unforgettable experience of encountering mountain gorillas in their natural habitat at Volcanoes National Park.

Begin your adventure with a guided exploration of Kigali, Rwanda’s bustling and clean capital. After airport pickup and hotel check-in, embark on a cultural city tour including visits to the Kigali Genocide Memorial, local markets, and artisan craft villages. Enjoy a traditional Rwandan lunch and explore Kigali’s art centers or cultural villages in the afternoon. End the day with dinner at a local restaurant or your hotel and rest up for the next day’s adventure.

Depart early for Volcanoes National Park. After a briefing at the headquarters, begin your guided gorilla trekking adventure. Hike through dense rainforest terrain until you encounter a habituated gorilla family, where you will spend up to one magical hour observing them in their natural environment. After the trek, return to your lodge for lunch, then drive back to Kigali. Depending on your schedule, enjoy some final sightseeing or shopping before your departure.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Volcanoes National Park, and transport within Kigali.
  • Guided Tours: Expert guide services for both the city tour and gorilla trekking experience.
  • Park Fees: Gorilla trekking permits and entrance fees are fully covered.
  • Meals: Lunch and refreshments on both days, with dinner included on Day 1.
  • Accommodation: Overnight stay in Kigali and at a lodge near Volcanoes National Park.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Lightweight attire for the city and trekking gear for the hike.
  • Hiking Boots: Sturdy, waterproof boots are a must for trekking.
  • Rain Gear: A rain jacket and pants are essential due to unpredictable weather.
  • Protective Clothing: Long sleeves and pants to protect from vegetation and insects.
  • Camera & Binoculars: To capture the stunning views and wildlife.
  • Fitness Level: Moderate fitness is recommended. Guides will assist throughout the trek.
  • Permits: Gorilla trekking permits must be booked in advance—availability is limited.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow current vaccination and health recommendations for a smooth journey.
