Lake Kivu Getaway

Three-Day Lake Kivu Lakeside and Cultural Adventure

Enjoy a peaceful retreat at Lake Kivu with boat cruises, tea plantation visits, and cultural experiences on this 3-day lakeside tour.

Arrive at Lake Kivu and check into your comfortable lakeside accommodation. Begin your stay with a leisurely boat cruise on Lake Kivu, taking in the stunning views of the calm waters and rolling hills. Enjoy a visit to one of the small islands on the lake, where you can explore and appreciate the peaceful surroundings.

After your boat cruise, spend the rest of the day relaxing by the lake. Engage in activities such as swimming, sunbathing, or strolling along the shoreline. In the evening, enjoy a delicious dinner at a lakeside restaurant or your accommodation, and unwind as you watch the sunset over the lake.

After breakfast, embark on a visit to one of the renowned tea plantations in the Lake Kivu region. Take a guided tour of the tea estates, where you will learn about the cultivation and processing of tea. Discover the traditional methods used in tea production and enjoy the scenic beauty of the lush tea fields.

Following your tea plantation visit, return to the lakeside for a relaxing afternoon. You may choose to explore local markets, interact with the local communities, or simply enjoy the tranquil environment by the lake. In the evening, savor another delightful dinner with lakeside views, reflecting on the day‚Äôs experiences.

On your final day, enjoy a relaxed breakfast before checking out of your accommodation. Spend the morning exploring any remaining attractions around Lake Kivu or enjoying additional lakeside activities. You might choose to take a short hike, visit nearby villages, or simply relax by the lake.

In the afternoon, begin your journey back to Kigali. Along the way, take in the scenic beauty of the Rwandan countryside and enjoy any last-minute stops at picturesque viewpoints. Arrive in Kigali and prepare for your departure or continue with your travel plans.
