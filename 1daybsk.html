Explore the Majestic Mount Bisoke on a Thrilling 1-Day Hiking Adventure

Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Rwanda with our 1-day Bisoke Hiking Tour. This adventure offers an immersive trek to Mount Bisoke, one of the iconic volcanoes in the Virunga Mountain range. Enjoy stunning views, diverse wildlife, and the serene beauty of the crater lake at the summit.

Mount Bisoke Hiking Tour

One-Day Mount Bisoke Hike

Embark on an unforgettable one-day hike to the summit of Mount Bisoke, where you'll witness stunning views of the crater lake and the surrounding volcanic landscapes.

Start your day with an early breakfast at your lodge or hotel. Afterward, you'll be transferred to Volcanoes National Park. Upon arrival at the park headquarters, meet your guide, who will brief you on the trek, including safety protocols, the trail, and what to expect on your journey.

The hike begins at the park headquarters and takes you through diverse landscapes, including forests and grasslands. As you ascend, enjoy the views and wildlife sightings. Upon reaching the summit, you will be rewarded with a breathtaking view of the crater lake, nestled within the volcanic caldera. The summit offers fantastic photo opportunities and a chance to relax while taking in the serene surroundings.

After enjoying the summit, begin your descent back to the park headquarters. The return trek offers different views and wildlife encounters. Upon arrival at the park headquarters, enjoy a packed lunch or meal at a nearby lodge. Afterward, you will be transferred back to Kigali, with some time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing before your departure.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Volcanoes National Park.
  • Guided Trek: Services of an experienced guide for the Mount Bisoke hike.
  • Park Fees: Entrance fees to Volcanoes National Park and hiking permits.
  • Meals: Packed lunch and refreshments during the hike.
  • Equipment: Basic hiking gear and support.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Dress in layers suitable for hiking and changing weather conditions.
  • Hiking Boots: Wear sturdy and comfortable footwear suitable for trekking.
  • Rain Gear: Waterproof jacket and pants for unpredictable weather.
  • Protective Clothing: Long sleeves and pants for protection from vegetation and insects.
  • Camera & Binoculars: Capture the scenic beauty and wildlife.
  • Fitness Level: Moderate fitness is recommended for the hike, which involves both ascent and descent through varied terrain.
  • Permits: Advance booking of permits is required. Ensure your reservations are confirmed before your visit.
  • Health Guidelines: Adhere to health and vaccination guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
