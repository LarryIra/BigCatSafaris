Akagera National Park

Akagera National Park, located in eastern Rwanda, is renowned for its diverse wildlife and breathtaking landscapes. The park is home to a wide range of animals, including the Big Five, antelopes, hippos, crocodiles, and over 480 bird species. It offers a unique safari experience with its open savannahs, lakes, and swamps.

Safari Packages

One-Day Wildlife Safari

Experience a thrilling one-day wildlife safari in Akagera National Park. Enjoy game viewing in the savanna, where you'll encounter the Big Five, antelopes, hippos, crocodiles, and a myriad of bird species. Take in the breathtaking views of the rolling hills and wetlands.

Your adventure begins with an early morning meet-up with a professional safari guide in Kigali. The 2.5-hour drive to the park sets the stage for your wildlife encounters. After park registration, witness herds of animals, including buffalo, elephants, rhinos, lions, leopards, hippos, crocodiles, antelopes, giraffes, zebras, and an array of beautiful birds. Optionally, enjoy a boat safari in the park before returning to Kigali.
