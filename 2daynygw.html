Explore the Thrills of Nyungwe Forest with Our Canopy Walkway Tour

Immerse yourself in the lush beauty of Nyungwe Forest National Park with our exhilarating 1-day canopy walkway tour. This adventure offers a unique perspective of Rwanda’s rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes, combining a canopy walk with a chance to explore the forest’s diverse flora and fauna.

Nyungwe Forest National Park

One-Day Nyungwe Forest Adventure

Discover the ancient rainforest of Nyungwe Forest with a canopy walk and nature trails, perfect for wildlife enthusiasts and adventurers.

Begin your day with a scenic drive from Kigali to Nyungwe Forest National Park. Known for its rich biodiversity and lush landscapes, Nyungwe offers a perfect getaway into Rwanda's ancient rainforest.

Upon arrival, embark on the thrilling canopy walkway, suspended high above the forest floor. Enjoy panoramic views of the dense forest canopy while keeping an eye out for the park's diverse birdlife and primates. The walk offers a bird’s-eye view of Nyungwe's lush vegetation and wildlife, providing great photo opportunities.

After the canopy walk, you’ll have the chance to explore more of Nyungwe through one of its well-maintained nature trails, such as the Igishigishigi Trail or Waterfall Trail. The trails offer the opportunity to experience the rainforest’s beauty up close and spot more wildlife. After your nature walk, enjoy a packed lunch before starting your drive back to Kigali.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Nyungwe Forest National Park.
  • Guided Tours: Expert guide services for the canopy walkway and nature walks.
  • Entrance Fees: Park entry fees and canopy walkway charges.
  • Meals: Packed lunch during the tour.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Lightweight, breathable clothing suitable for hiking and forest exploration.
  • Hiking Boots: Sturdy footwear for walking on forest trails.
  • Rain Gear: Waterproof jacket and pants in case of rain.
  • Sun Protection: Hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen for sun protection.
  • Camera & Binoculars: Capture stunning views and wildlife sightings.
  • Fitness Level: Moderate fitness is recommended for walking and climbing the canopy walkway.
  • Permits: No specific permits required beyond park entrance fees.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow health and vaccination guidelines for a safe and enjoyable experience.
