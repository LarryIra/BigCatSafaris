Discover the Untamed Beauty of Rwanda’s Premier Wildlife Reserve

Embark on an exhilarating 1-day safari in Akagera National Park, Rwanda’s premier wildlife reserve. This action-packed adventure offers a chance to explore diverse ecosystems, spot magnificent wildlife, and experience the natural beauty of Rwanda in a single day.

Wildlife Safari

1-Day Akagera Wildlife Safari

Experience Rwanda’s only Big Five safari destination in just one day. Akagera National Park offers breathtaking landscapes and a rich variety of wildlife — perfect for adventurers short on time but big on exploration.

Morning: Begin with an early morning pickup from your Kigali accommodation or airport. Enjoy a scenic 2.5-hour drive to Akagera National Park.

Arrival & Briefing: Meet your expert safari guide at the park entrance and receive a safety and conservation briefing.

Game Drive: Venture into the park’s savannas and woodlands in search of the Big Five—lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino. Look out for giraffes, zebras, antelopes, and a variety of birds along the way.

Lunch: Stop for a picnic lunch at a scenic viewpoint within the park. Savor traditional Rwandan dishes surrounded by nature.

Boat Safari: Cruise along Lake Ihema and spot hippos, crocodiles, and aquatic birds. This peaceful boat ride provides an entirely different perspective of the park’s ecosystems.

Afternoon: Continue your game drive through other regions of the park. With the help of your guide, try to spot any animals you may have missed earlier.

Return to Kigali: Begin your journey back to the city, arriving in the late afternoon or early evening, full of unforgettable memories.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Kigali to Akagera National Park in a comfortable 4x4 vehicle.
  • Guided Safari: Expert guide for both game drives and boat safari.
  • Meals: Picnic lunch and refreshments.
  • Park Fees: Entrance fees and boat safari charges included.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Light and breathable clothes ideal for the heat.
  • Footwear: Comfortable shoes for short walks and getting in/out of the vehicle.
  • Sun Protection: Sunglasses, hat, and sunscreen are essential.
  • Camera & Binoculars: To capture wildlife and landscapes.
  • Water Bottle: Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Fitness Level: Suitable for all fitness levels—minimal physical exertion involved.
  • Wildlife Sightings: While sightings are not guaranteed, Akagera is home to a diverse and abundant wildlife population.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow all health and safety recommendations including vaccinations and hygiene practices.
