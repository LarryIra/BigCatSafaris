Explore the Wonders of Rwanda with Our 4-Day Volcanoes National Park Adventure

Embark on a captivating 4-day journey through Rwanda, featuring a blend of gorilla trekking, golden monkey trekking, a visit to the Dian Fossey Tomb, and cultural excursions. Begin with a guided city tour of Kigali before delving into the natural and cultural marvels of Volcanoes National Park.

Safari Experience

4-Day Gorilla Trekking Safari

A four-day immersive journey combining wildlife, culture, and conservation. Explore Kigali’s vibrant cityscape, trek to see gorillas and golden monkeys, and walk in the footsteps of Dian Fossey.

Upon arrival in Kigali, our representative will warmly welcome you. Begin your adventure with a city tour, visiting the Kigali Genocide Memorial and exploring local markets and craft villages. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before taking a scenic drive to Volcanoes National Park. Check into your lodge or hotel near the park and relax for the evening.

After an early breakfast, head to the park headquarters for a gorilla trekking briefing. Trek through the rainforest to locate a habituated gorilla family and spend up to one hour observing them. After lunch, visit the Gorilla Guardian Village for a cultural experience featuring traditional music, dance, and crafts.

Begin the day with breakfast, then head out for golden monkey trekking. Observe these energetic primates for up to one hour in the forest. After lunch, visit the Ellen Campus to learn about conservation and community development efforts in the region.

After breakfast, embark on a guided hike to the Dian Fossey Tomb, learning about her legacy in gorilla conservation. Enjoy lunch at the lodge before your return drive to Kigali. Depending on your flight time, enjoy some final shopping or city exploration. Transfer to Kigali International Airport for departure or optional overnight stay.
  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers between Kigali and Volcanoes National Park, plus city transfers.
  • Guided Treks: Gorilla trekking, golden monkey trekking, and Dian Fossey hike with expert guides.
  • Park Fees: Permits for all treks and park entrance fees included.
  • Meals: All meals during the safari including lunch during excursions and dinner on arrival day.
  • Accommodation: Three nights near Volcanoes National Park, and one optional night in Kigali if extended stay is chosen.
  • Comfortable Clothing: Suitable for both city touring and forest trekking.
  • Hiking Boots: Durable footwear for walking on uneven trails.
  • Rain Gear: Weather can be unpredictable—bring waterproof jackets and pants.
  • Protective Clothing: Long sleeves and pants recommended to protect from insects and vegetation.
  • Camera and Binoculars: Essential for wildlife viewing and capturing memories.
  • Fitness Level: A moderate level of fitness is recommended for trekking and hiking.
  • Permits: Book your gorilla and monkey trekking permits well in advance.
  • Health Guidelines: Follow all travel health recommendations for a safe and enjoyable safari.
